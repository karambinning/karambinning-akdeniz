The Kitchen

Charcoal, lamb, smoke. Nothing hides on a mangal grill.

We opened in 2013 as Merhaba. The grill never changed, the welcome never changed, and we have been carrying both forward as Akdeniz ever since — meze brought to the table, raki when you want it, fine wines when you don't.

Cosy, unassuming, and recognised by the British Kebab Awards in 2018. A neighbourhood Turkish restaurant on East Road, here for lunch and for dinner.
